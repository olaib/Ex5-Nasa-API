Instruction Page:

with this app, you can browse a NASA APOD (astronomy picture of the day) service and image viewer from space.
to get started, follow the steps:

  1. First select a date from the calendar, so you can see photos or videos from the selected day till today
  2. Click the search button and the photos/videos will be shown
  3. You can click the 'description' button to see the description of the selected photos
  4. You can click the 'comments' button to see others comments of this photo
  5. Simply enter your comment in the text field then click the "pos" button to share it
  6. Your comment will be added to the comments section, and you can simply delete it
  7. You can click on the image to see it on full screen if it is a video you can click on 'full screen' button
